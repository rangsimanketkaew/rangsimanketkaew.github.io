My running journey begins when my younger friend introduce me to join Adidas Running Club Zurich (ARZ) in 2023. 
The first time I ran with ARZ people was in the morning session (7.00 - 8.00). The course was about 8 km, mixed train 
of city and hill, with 200 m elevation. On that day, my pace was about 7 minutes per km and I had an abdominal stitch 
(side cramps) while in running after 3 km. It was so painful and I suffered a lot! 
</p>
